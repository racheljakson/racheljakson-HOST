Privacy Policy — Last updated May 2026

HOST is an event planning and décor preview app. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use HOST.

Information we collect

  • Photos you upload of your space
  • Wishlist and mood board content you create within the app
  • Pinterest account data you choose to connect, including boards and pins you have saved
  • Basic usage data to improve app performance

How we use your information

  • To display and preview décor items in your uploaded room photos
  • To save and organize your wishlists, mood boards, and shopping lists
  • To pull Pinterest inspiration content you have authorized us to access
  • To improve the HOST app experience

Pinterest data

If you choose to connect your Pinterest account, HOST accesses your boards and pins solely to display inspiration content within the app. We do not store, sell, or share your Pinterest data with third parties. You can disconnect your Pinterest account at any time.

Photos and room images

Photos you upload are used only to generate room previews within the app. We do not share your photos with third parties or use them for advertising purposes.

Data sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to anyone. We do not share your data with advertisers. Data may be shared only where required by law.

Data retention

You may delete your account and associated data at any time by contacting us. Upon deletion, your data is permanently removed from our systems.

Children's privacy

HOST is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes through the app.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: hello@hostapp.com
